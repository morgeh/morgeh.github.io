Micro Fusion Bonds

A classic reliable method.

U Tip hair is used for this method. It is one of the most popular methods with up to 60% of the market using this technique. It is suitable for all hair types. Hair that is pre-bonded is used for this method as it doesn't shed.

Micro fusion is a hot system that does not damage the natural hair. It can last up to 3 - 4 months depending on how well they are maintained.

This method creates a freefall natural effect great for finer weak hair.

Micro Rings

Micro rings are made from metal (copper/aluminium).

This method should not be used on metal allergy sufferers.

Micro rings use no glue, heat or adhesives, they are a completely cold system. They should last about 3 - 4 months if maintained well.

Micro rings can be repositioned/pushed up every 4 - 6 weeks depending on hair growth providing a ver cost effective hair extension method.

Micro rings are great for hot climates because they remain secure and strong.

Shrinkies

Shrinkies are small shrink wrap tubes. They are a cross between fusion bonds and micro rings.

Shrinkies are extremely light weight and gentle. Superb for thin hair.

They are heat activated so not suitable for holidays.

They should last up to 3 months if maintained well. This method will suit everyone.

Nano Rings

Nano rings are made from copper/aluminium. It is the newest method on the market.

It is virtually undetectable and is the smallest hair extension on the market, the size of a pen nib!

They last for 3 - 4 months if maintained well. Highly comfortable against the scalp and whilst sleeping unlike other bulkier methods.

Very gentle and non-damaging to the natural hair Nano rings are currently worn by 90% of UK Celebrities.
